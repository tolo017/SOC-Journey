# SOC-Journey
My 40-day journey in to becoming a junior SOC analyst.


## Day 1: Mantra
` A SOC analyst sees a ping reply and thinks 'inventory'. A Black Hat sees a ping reply and thinks 'opportunity'.`

## Day 2: Mantra
` The Linux filesystem is a map. /etc is the brain, /var is the lungs, /home is the jewelry box. If you do not know the map, you are just a burglar stumbling in the dark.`

## Day 3: Mantra
` Permissions are the bouncers of the Linux club. Read? You can see the dance floor. Write? You can change the music. Execute? You can walk through the door. But SUID? That's the bouncer handing you his VIP badge and saying 'Go ahead, act like me.' If I find a misplaced SUID binary, I don't need to break root. I just borrow the bouncer's jacket.`
